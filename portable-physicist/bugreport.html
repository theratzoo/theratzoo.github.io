Reporting a bug



How to Report a Bug

The most important thing to do when reporting a bug is be as detailed as possible. The key details to include are the bug itself and how you got to the bug. Including your current settings are also helpful if they may have produced the bug. Screenshots of the bug itself are also helpful in us solving it in a timely matter.
