I expect to be working on really fleshing out the story and characters along with beginning the first stages of coding (classes, function prototypes, etc.) within the next couple months. By the summer, I hope to be able to give a larger update on the video game, including information on the specifics. But for now, as everything is tentative (and I would rather keep it quiet to protect the ideas), I cannot really post details. However, if you wish to discuss the game or help with ideas, I would greatly appreciate it- just contact me through one of my social medias!
